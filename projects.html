TASIYICI 2024

TASIYICI, is a semi-autonomous wheelchair. It specifically tackled the issue of transportation that disabled people faced indoors. In areas like hospitals, libraries etc. it enabled easier pathfinding and transportation. Here are the specs:

  • Mechanum Wheels for strafing and easier maneuverability
  • Limelight Sensor for pathfinding and obstacle recognition

It became a Finalist on TeknoFest (One of Turkey's biggest Science Fairs)

Venator 2023

Venator is one of the most meaningful projects of my life. As the first major project I've led, it became a turning point in our team's history. Venator broke what we thought was a curse on our team, propelling us back to the top of the charts. Our hard work was rewarded when we earned Second Place in the Halic Regional competition. Here are the Specs:

  • Custom Manufactured Swerve Drive
  • One Stage 2 Side Riging Elevator
  • A Cascade extender
  • A pneumatic intake mechanism for versatile object picking
